<p><strong>The Raman Fiber Amplifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lumped</li><li>Distributed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Raman fiber amplifier market is primarily segmented into lumped and distributed types. Lumped Raman amplifiers use a concentrated fiber segment for amplification, offering simplicity and ease of integration, making them ideal for short-distance applications. In contrast, distributed Raman amplifiers utilize a longer length of fiber for gain distribution along the entire link, enhancing performance over longer distances and improving signal quality. Both types serve distinct needs in optical communication, catering to varying levels of complexity and application requirements.</p></p>
