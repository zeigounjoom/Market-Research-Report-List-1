<p><p>The Redox Flow Battery market primarily consists of two types: Vanadium Redox Flow Batteries (VRFB) and Hybrid Flow Batteries. VRFBs use vanadium ions in both positive and negative electrolytes, offering long cycle life and scalability, ideal for large-scale energy storage. Hybrid Flow Batteries combine characteristics of traditional batteries and flow batteries, utilizing different chemistries to enhance efficiency and performance. These systems are gaining traction for applications in renewable energy integration, grid stability, and electric vehicle charging infrastructure.</p></p>
