<p><p>The undercurrent relay market is categorized into two main types: automatic reset and manual reset. Automatic reset relays reset themselves after an overload condition is cleared, ensuring continuous operation without manual intervention. This type is ideal for applications requiring minimal downtime. In contrast, manual reset relays require an operator to manually reset the system after an overload, providing an extra layer of control and safety. Both types serve critical roles in protecting equipment in various industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
