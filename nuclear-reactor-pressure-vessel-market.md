<p><p>The Nuclear Reactor Pressure Vessel Market encompasses various types of reactors, each with distinct designs and operating principles. Pressurized Water Reactors (PWR) use pressurized water as both coolant and moderator, while Boiling Water Reactors (BWR) allow water to boil within the reactor vessel. Pressurized Heavy Water Reactors (PHWR) utilize heavy water for moderation and cooling. Gas-cooled Reactors (GCR) use gas as a coolant, while Low-Temperature Gas-cooled Reactors (LWGR) operate at lower temperatures. Fast Breeder Reactors (FBR) generate more fuel than they consume, enhancing sustainability.</p></p>
