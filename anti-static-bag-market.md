<p><p>The anti-static bag market is segmented into metal anti-static bags and non-metallic anti-static bags. Metal anti-static bags, often made from materials like aluminum foil, provide excellent electromagnetic shielding and are ideal for high-sensitivity electronic components. Non-metallic anti-static bags, usually composed of polyethylene or polyester, utilize static-dissipative properties without metal, making them lightweight and cost-effective, suitable for various electronic products. Both types play a crucial role in protecting sensitive electronics from electrostatic discharge during storage and transportation.</p></p>
